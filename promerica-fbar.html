Promerica FBAR

US persons with foreign bank accounts exceeding $10,000 at any point during the year must file an FBAR (FinCEN Form 114). The key figure required per account is the highest balance held at any point during the calendar year — not the year-end balance. Drop in one or more Promerica monthly PDF statements and this tool scans the Saldo Acumulado (running balance) column across all of them to surface that number.
